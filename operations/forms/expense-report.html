Expense Report

Submit expenses without the spreadsheet mess.

Enter personal business expenses, mark which receipts include GST, then download a clean report for Finance. Attach receipt images or PDFs to the same email when you send it in.

Nothing is saved here. This page only uses what is on your screen. Download or print the report, then clear it before the next person uses the Hub.
Step 01

Employee and report details

Use the notes field for anything Finance should know, like missing receipts, client names, or which event/trip the expenses belong to.

Step 02

Expense lines

Enter the full amount you personally paid. If GST is included, the tool estimates the GST portion at 5/105 of the receipt total. Use Manual GST when the receipt shows a specific GST amount.
