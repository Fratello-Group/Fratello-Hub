Purpose

To define standard operating procedures for managing inventory movements, receiving, and shipping of all coffee and packaging materials using the Odoo ERP system, while ensuring CFIA-compliant traceability, inventory accuracy, and fulfillment integrity under Fratello Coffee Roasters' Preventive Control Plan (PCP).

Scope

Applies to all warehouse staff, logistics coordinators, and operations team members involved in:

  • Receiving raw materials (green coffee, packaging, ingredients)
  • Managing internal stock movements and lot assignments
  • Shipping finished products to customers and distributors

Related CFIA SOPs and System Documents

  • SOP-PCP-5.1 – Receiving Procedure
  • SOP-PCP-5.2 – Shipping Procedure
  • SOP-PCP-5.6 – Traceability and Control

1. Product Receiving (Odoo Ref: pg. 95–99)

Step-by-Step

  • Access Inventory > Receipts in Odoo
  • Locate PO for expected incoming goods
  • Visually inspect all materials (follow Receiving SOP)
  • Enter actual received quantity and assign or verify Lot Numbers
  • Validate receipt to update stock in Odoo

Food Safety Verification

  • Confirm COAs and packaging integrity (Incoming Input Controls)
  • Reject or hold any damaged or questionable products (Hold SOP)
  • Record supplier lot and internal lot for traceability

2. Internal Warehouse Transfers (Odoo Ref: pg. 100–103)

Common Scenarios

  • Green coffee from receiving bay to silo staging
  • Roasted coffee from production to packaging station
  • Finished goods to storage or shipping staging

Step-by-Step

  • Navigate to Inventory > Internal Transfers
  • Select product and specify origin/destination locations
  • Enter Lot Number for traceability
  • Confirm move in Odoo

Controls

  • All transfers must maintain product identity (no commingling lots)
  • Use physical tags if transferring between areas temporarily

3. Inventory Adjustments & Cycle Counts

When to Use

  • Stock discrepancy
  • Physical count correction
  • Damaged/lost product

Procedure

  • Go to Inventory > Adjustments
  • Select product and location
  • Record reason and update lot quantity
  • Submit for QA or supervisor approval

Recordkeeping

  • Must be logged in Inventory Adjustment Log
  • Significant losses require incident review (Corrective Action SOP)

4. Outbound Shipping (Odoo Ref: pg. 126–132)

Step-by-Step

  • Navigate to Sales > Delivery Orders
  • Match against active Sales Orders (SOs)
  • Select correct finished goods Lot Numbers
  • Pick, pack, and confirm shipment quantities
  • Validate shipment to deduct inventory

Verification & Controls

  • Use shipping checklist to ensure:
    • Correct customer and order
    • Lot codes match traceability records
    • Labels present and legible
  • Maintain lot-level traceability for all shipments

5. Records and Logs

Record/Form Location
Receiving Logs Odoo + Receiving SOP Checklist
Internal Transfer Logs Odoo > Inventory > Operations
Inventory Adjustment Logs Digital or Physical Binder
Shipping Logs Odoo > Sales > Delivery Orders
Traceability Reports Odoo Smart Buttons > Lot Tree

CFIA Compliance Highlights

  • Supports SFCR Part 4 – Preventive Controls: Inventory control, segregation, damage protocols
  • Fulfills Part 5 – Traceability (lot-level trace in/out)
  • Tied to supplier approval, label control, and deviation management
