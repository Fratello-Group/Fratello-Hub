Purpose

To ensure all Fratello employees, contractors, and applicable visitors are trained in food safety, GMPs, hygiene, and site-specific standard operating procedures (SOPs). This supports compliance with SFCR Part 4 (Preventive Controls) by ensuring personnel are competent and aware of their roles in maintaining food safety and quality.

Scope

Applies to:

  • All full-time, part-time, temporary, and seasonal staff
  • Any contractor or visitor performing work or entering designated food production/storage zones

Responsibility

RoleResponsibility
Director of OperationsOversees training program and approves SOPs
Department Leads/SupervisorsConduct role-specific and hands-on training
All EmployeesMust complete and adhere to training requirements

Training Timeline & Structure

1. Orientation (Day 1) – Mandatory Before Starting Production Work

Includes:

  • Review of the Employee Hygiene & GMP SOP
  • Proper handwashing demonstration and verification (aligned with SOP)
  • Tour of all relevant zones (identify hand wash, sanitizer, mop/service room locations)
  • Overview of:
    • Sanitation expectations
    • Allergen Awareness
    • Glass & Brittle Plastic Policy
    • Pest Prevention Basics
    • Security & Visitor Policy
  • Completion of the Personal Hygiene Training Quiz

All orientation training is recorded in the Training Record and signed by the employee.

2. First 30 Days – On-the-Job & Observational Training

TopicMethod
Job-specific SOPsShadowing, buddy system, practical demonstration
Food Safety & Cross-contamination ControlReinforcement from Sanitation and Pre-Op Procedures
Hold, Deviation, and Contaminated Product ResponseRefer to Returned, Damaged, or Suspect Product SOP
Equipment sanitation & tool cleaningDirect reference to Sanitation SOP (Procedures A–C)
Glass, Brittle Plastic, Knife ControlObservation and reporting protocols reviewed
Pest Reporting ProtocolIntroduction to Pest Control SOP and who to contact

Training performance is reviewed and documented on the Training Checklist.

3. Annual Refresher Training

Conducted for all staff in the following areas:

  • Personal Hygiene & GMP Policy
  • Sanitation SSOPs
  • HACCP (for HACCP Team)
  • Allergen Control
  • Glass & Brittle Plastic Awareness
  • Food Security (Security SOPs and suspicious activity response)

Refresher training is recorded and reviewed during internal audits.

Additional Notes

Sanitation Role Training

All staff involved in sanitation must be trained in:

  • Cintas chemical handling and labeling
  • Dry vs. wet cleaning methods
  • Sink cleaning (3-compartment method)
  • PPE usage
  • Verification and sanitation tool care

See: Sanitation SOP and Master Sanitation Schedule

Multilingual & Literacy Considerations

  • Currently, all employees must be proficient in English
  • If future needs arise, SOPs will be adapted with visuals, translations, or oral instruction
