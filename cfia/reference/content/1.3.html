Purpose

To define how Fratello Coffee Roasters conducts internal audits and verification activities across its food safety programs to ensure compliance with the Safe Food for Canadians Regulations (SFCR) and the effectiveness of its Preventive Control Plan (PCP).

Scope

Applies to all Fratello food safety, traceability, hygiene, maintenance, documentation, and regulatory programs listed in the PCP and associated review checklist.

Objectives

  • Confirm ongoing compliance with CFIA and SFCR requirements
  • Ensure SOPs are followed and implemented effectively
  • Identify non-conformities or areas for improvement
  • Maintain records that support traceability, recalls, and inspections

Frequency of Internal Audits

Audit Type Frequency
Targeted Program Audits Quarterly (rotating schedule based on risk and activity)
Full System PCP Audit Annually
Ad Hoc Audit As needed (customer complaints, deviations, mock recall failures)

Responsibility

Role Responsibility
QA or Audit Lead Plans and executes internal audits; completes audit forms
Area Supervisors Cooperate during audits; respond to findings
Director of Operations Reviews results and signs off on major corrective actions

Audit Process

  • Schedule audit per quarterly plan or trigger event
  • Use the Fratello CFIA Program Review Checklist with Frequency Assignments as the baseline
  • Select documents, records, or staff interviews as part of the verification
  • Record findings using the Internal Audit Record Form (Appendix A)
  • Identify and classify any non-conformances:
    • Minor (documentation, training lapse)
    • Major (missed traceability, unlabeled product, pest activity)
  • Assign Corrective Action Request (CAR) if needed
  • Re-audit or confirm closure within required timeframe (typically 30 days)

Verification Activities

  • Review of logs, checklists, and SOP sign-offs
  • Confirmation of traceability using mock lot trace
  • Inspection of facilities, sanitation records, and pest control logs
  • Label checks, product review, training record review
  • Comparison of on-floor practices with written SOPs

Records Retention

Record Retention Period
Internal Audit Record Form 2 years
Corrective Action Requests 2 years
Program Review Checklists 2 years
