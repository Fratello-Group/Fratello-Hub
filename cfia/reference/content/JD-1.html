Summary

The Packaging Team Member is responsible for packaging and labelling Fratello Coffee Roasters coffee — ensuring customers are presented a consistent, high-quality product every time.

Job titlePackaging Team Member
Reports toDirector of Production / Warehouse Supervisor
Direct reportsNone

Areas of responsibility — packaging

  • Package the daily schedule of roasted coffee in the required package sizes.
  • Identify production priority and needs based on the Daily Production Schedule.
  • Manually record Build Assembly on the daily production schedule; create and post Build Assembly in QuickBooks, ensuring accuracy.
  • Accurately weigh, fill and heat-seal coffee bags following specifications.
  • Perform numerous visual, weight and seal inspections of individual bags during operation.
  • Adjust box-erector equipment to handle the appropriate case size.
  • Close, label and tape boxes; move boxes from the conveyor onto pallets, stacking appropriately.
  • Grind coffee as specified on the daily schedule.
  • Neatly apply labels; ensure case printing is accurate and follows the packaging standards and procedure.
  • Notify the Warehouse Supervisor of low quantities of roasted coffee, labels or packaging materials, or an incomplete build assembly.
  • Cover leftover coffee if needed; roll empty bins back to the roasting department.
  • Responsible for stocking teas; pre-label bags using the label machine when packaging and assemblies are complete.
  • Assist Warehouse Team Members as required; maintain accurate production and inventory records.
  • Maintain a safe, clean and organized work environment; follow the sanitation standards and schedule.

Experience & qualifications

  • 1+ years of experience in warehouse and/or distribution.
  • High degree of accuracy and attention to detail; proficient general math skills.
  • Appreciation for clean, organized work environments; thrives in a team.
  • Effective oral and written communication and time-management skills.
