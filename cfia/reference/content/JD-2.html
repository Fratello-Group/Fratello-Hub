Summary

The Packaging Machine Operator is responsible for packaging and labelling pre-packaged Fratello Coffee Roasters coffee using the portion-pack machine — ensuring a consistent, high-quality product every time.

Job titlePackaging Machine Operator
Reports toDirector of Production / Warehouse Supervisor
Direct reportsNone

Portion-pack machine operation

  • Operate, monitor and maintain the portion-pack machine, including all preventive-maintenance schedules.
  • Operate, monitor and maintain the production coffee grinder, including its weekly cleaning schedule.
  • Calibrate and clean the portion-pack machine; ensure it is free of debris before use.
  • Adjust, test and prepare the machine to start a packaging cycle; feed coffee grounds and bagging materials and monitor while running.
  • Manage product size change-over and track product flow.
  • Regularly check packaging quality to spot machine-related flaws; inspect for defects and adjust settings to minimize waste.
  • Ensure the nitrogen-flush system displaces the correct amount of oxygen; fix issues during operation.
  • Strict adherence to all company policies, procedures and safety rules; test machine operation periodically per the PM schedule.

Packaging

  • Package the daily schedule of roasted coffee in the required package sizes.
  • Manually record Build Assembly; create and post in QuickBooks, ensuring accuracy.
  • Accurately weigh, fill and heat-seal coffee bags following specifications; set up machinery (film/corrugate/tape/label printing).
  • Validate all finished-product quality parameters: fill weight, code date, package length, bag count, oxygen level.
  • Form corrugate into cases; stack full, sealed cases into the correct pallet configuration per packing slips.
  • Shrink-wrap and store surplus pallets with an attached inventory sheet; stage pallets to receive finished cases.
  • Notify the Warehouse Supervisor of low quantities of roasted coffee, labels or packaging materials.
  • Assist Warehouse and Packaging Team Members as required; maintain a safe, clean, organized work environment and follow the sanitation standards.

Experience & qualifications

  • 1+ years of experience as a machine operator.
