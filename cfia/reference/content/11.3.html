Purpose

This document outlines the Critical Control Points (CCPs) identified throughout Fratello Coffee Roasters' production process. The table ensures compliance with food safety regulations by identifying potential hazards, control measures, and monitoring protocols at each stage.

CCP Summary Table

Step in Process Hazard Type Hazard Description CCP? Control Measure Critical Limit / Specification Monitoring Procedure Corrective Action Verification Activities Records Maintained
Green Coffee Receiving Physical Foreign material (e.g., stones, debris) No Visual inspection at receiving No visible contamination Warehouse staff visual inspection Reject or clean lot if foreign matter found Internal audits and random inspections Receiving Log, Inspection Reports
Silo Loading (through screen & magnet) Physical Metal fragments, dust Yes Screen and magnet filtration No metallic particles detected Check magnet integrity monthly Stop process, inspect, clean magnets Magnet verification checklist monthly Magnet Cleaning Log, Maintenance Record
Roasting Biological/Physical Undercooking (microbial survival), fire hazard Yes Temperature/time control; fire SOP Roaster reaches ≥ 180°C internally Digital probe temperature logging Re-roast or discard; follow fire response SOP Roast log review, fire drill reports Roast Logs, Fire Safety Training Records
Cooling in tray Physical Contaminants introduced via air or environment No Regular cleaning of cooling tray No visible contaminants Visual inspection post-cooling Clean tray; discard contaminated batch Sanitation verification log Cooling Tray Cleaning Log
Destoning Physical Stones, dense foreign material Yes Vibratory separator + magnets No stones or metal in output Daily magnet and separator inspection Remove contaminated coffee, investigate source Daily checks documented Destoner Log, Magnet Cleaning Record
Packaging (coffee & tea) Physical/Chemical Contaminants in packaging area; incorrect labeling Yes Magnet check; label verification SOP No foreign objects; label must match Visual label checks; magnet sweep before run Stop line, relabel, recheck labels and magnets Weekly label audit, pre-op inspection logs Label Check Log, Magnet Inspection Record
Tea Packaging (Idle Tea) Physical/Chemical Residues in machine; cross-contact allergens Yes Pre-op dry wipe, full batch isolation All residue removed; correct label Pre-op inspection with dry cloth Reclean, isolate partial batch, relabel Supervisor sign-off on inspection Tea Packaging Log, Sanitation Checklists

Review Frequency: Annually or upon change to process or equipment.
