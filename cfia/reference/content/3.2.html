Purpose

To define and control all steps in Fratello Coffee Roasters' production workflow, from receiving green beans to final product storage and dispatch, ensuring food safety, quality, and regulatory compliance in accordance with the Safe Food for Canadians Regulations (SFCR).

Scope

Applies to all Fratello production staff and QA personnel involved in green bean intake, grading, roasting, grinding, packaging, warehousing, and shipment.

Process Descriptions and Controls

1. Green Coffee Receiving

  • Procedure: Verify packaging integrity, confirm documentation, record in Cropster.
  • Controls: CFIA import compliance; check for pests, mold, accurate lot numbers.
  • Linked SOPs: Receiving Procedure, Approved Supplier Program.

2. Grading and Sample Evaluation

  • Procedure: 200g grading; moisture, density, odor, defects; SCA protocol cupping.
  • Controls: Must meet Fratello quality thresholds (e.g., 86+ for single origin).
  • Linked SOPs: Sanitation SOP (sample tools), Training SOP.

3. Green Bean Silo Loading

  • Procedure: One lot per silo; chamber must be empty/clean.
  • Controls: Cross-contamination prevention, silo log entry, visual checks.
  • Linked SOPs: Glass & Brittle Plastic SOP, Premises Inspection SOP.

4. Roasting

  • Procedure: Run by profile via Cropster; chaff removed regularly.
  • Controls: Curve review; probe temp calibration; fire prevention.
  • Linked SOPs: Calibration SOP, Preventive Maintenance SOP.

5. Quality Assurance & Profiling

  • Procedure: Blind cupping, profile validation, solubility check.
  • Controls: Score threshold, QC records in Cropster.
  • Linked SOPs: Training SOP, QA Lab Procedure (future).

6. Grinding

  • Procedure: Confirm settings, ventilation, label grind.
  • Controls: CO exposure precautions, calibration, QA validation.
  • Linked SOPs: Preventive Maintenance SOP, Sanitation SOP.

7. Packaging

  • Procedure: Lot number and roast date labelled; bags sealed; boxes coded.
  • Controls: Label review; seal integrity; CoPilot printer verification.
  • Linked SOPs: Hold Product SOP, Traceability SOP, Label Control Policy.

8. Storage and Dispatch

  • Procedure: FIFO rotation; climate control; outgoing log entry.
  • Controls: Pest & temp monitoring; pallet separation.
  • Linked SOPs: Pest Control SOP, Security SOP, Shipping SOP.

Recordkeeping

  • All records logged in Cropster, manual logs, and QA forms.
  • Includes: Green intake logs, grading sheets, roasts, QC data, packaging logs.
  • Retention Period: 2 years minimum, per CFIA Part 5 (Traceability).

Regulatory & Quality Standards

  • Complies with SFCR Part 4 (Preventive Controls) and Part 5 (Traceability).
  • Cupping and grading aligned with SCA Green Coffee Standards.
