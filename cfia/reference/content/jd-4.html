Summary

The Warehouse Team Member completes pick-and-pack activities and prepares Fratello Coffee Roasters products for shipping and delivery — organized and detail-oriented to ensure Analog Café, wholesale and online customer orders are delivered accurately and on time.

Job titleWarehouse Team Member
Reports toWarehouse Supervisor
Direct reportsNone

Pick and pack

  • Print Sales Order packing slips from QuickBooks and complete scheduled orders, recording the last sales-order number.
  • Participate in inventory integrity and counts (weekly, monthly, quarterly, annually); notify the Warehouse Supervisor of orders missing a Build Assembly or of low PAR inventory.
  • Pack all orders to the wholesale and online packaging standards onto designated skids; shrink-wrap and store surplus pallets with an attached inventory sheet.
  • Maintain accurate inventory; retrieve correct products per the packing slips; email invoices with pick-up details or ETA tracking (every order has an invoice).
  • Monitor "work in process" inventory from packaging to finished-goods shelves; cycle coffee inventory FIFO; keep a safe, clean, organized work area per the sanitation standards.

Shipping

  • Organize and package daily shipments by courier, pickup and delivery time; determine the shipment method and prepare bills, invoices and shipping documents.
  • Load and unload goods; assist delivery drivers with loading/unloading.
  • Receive warehouse shipments, verify contents, and stock/label items into the appropriate locations.

Experience & qualifications

  • 1+ years in warehouse and/or distribution; familiar with fulfillment, shipping, receiving and general warehouse work.
  • High accuracy and attention to detail; proficient general math; effective communication and time-management; thrives in a team.
  • Proficient with Microsoft Word/Excel/Outlook and QuickBooks (or comparable).
