Purpose

To provide a visual representation of Fratello Coffee Roasters' process steps where food safety hazards are controlled through preventive measures, including identification of potential Critical Control Points (CCPs) and justification for each.

Process Flow with Hazard and CCP Analysis

1. Receiving Green Coffee

  • Hazards: Physical (stones, debris), Biological (mold, mycotoxins), Chemical (pesticide residues)
  • Controls: Supplier verification, incoming inspection, moisture content testing, traceability logging
  • CCP: No (controlled through prerequisite programs and supplier controls)

2. Green Coffee Storage

  • Hazards: Biological (pest contamination, mold growth)
  • Controls: Pest control program, environmental monitoring, FIFO system
  • CCP: No

3. Green Bean Loading (to Silos)

  • Hazards: Physical (debris, metal fragments)
  • Controls: Magnet system, screen filter, visual inspection
  • CCP: Yes (Magnet system – removal of metal)

4. Roasting

  • Hazards: Chemical (acrylamide formation), Biological (inadequate heat treatment), Physical (foreign material)
  • Controls: Time/temperature parameters, equipment calibration, preventive maintenance
  • CCP: Yes (Roasting step – biological hazard reduction)

5. Cooling & Destoning

  • Hazards: Physical (stones, metal)
  • Controls: Destoner, post-roast magnets, visual inspection
  • CCP: Yes (Post-roast magnet/destoner)

6. Packaging Preparation

  • Hazards: Physical (foreign material), Allergen (cross-contact – tea/coffee equipment)
  • Controls: Sanitation procedures, dry wipe cleaning, allergen separation (tea equipment)
  • CCP: No (controlled through sanitation SOPs)

7. Final Packaging (Coffee & Tea)

  • Hazards: Physical (foreign material), Labelling (incorrect information)
  • Controls: Final visual check, label verification procedure, magnet trap in packaging line
  • CCP: Yes (Magnet trap – physical hazard mitigation)

8. Storage of Finished Goods

  • Hazards: Biological (pests), Physical (contamination), Chemical (cleaning agent residues)
  • Controls: Pest control, chemical control procedures, designated clean storage area
  • CCP: No

9. Shipping

  • Hazards: Physical (package damage, contamination)
  • Controls: Visual inspection of packaging, transport verification, vehicle cleanliness
  • CCP: No

Legend

  • CCP: Critical Control Point identified for food safety
  • No CCP: Controlled by prerequisite programs or standard SOPs

Document Control

Reviewed and approved by QA Manager. Review Frequency: Annually or after any process change.
