1.0 Purpose

To outline the training requirements for all Fratello Coffee Roasters personnel involved in operations governed by the Preventive Control Plan (PCP), in compliance with SFCR 89 and sub-element 8.2.

2.0 Scope

Applies to all employees engaged in receiving, roasting, packaging, quality assurance, warehouse handling, and sanitation activities. It ensures all staff are trained to execute their duties safely and in compliance with SOPs outlined in the PCP.

3.0 Responsibilities

  • Supervisors: Ensure assigned employees are trained according to their job function.
  • QA & Training Coordinator: Maintain the master training schedule and audit completion records.
  • Employees: Participate in scheduled training and sign off on acknowledgments.

5.0 Training Records

  • All training events must be recorded using the Personnel Training Log Form.
  • Signed acknowledgments are filed in the Section 8.3 – Training Binder.
  • Digital versions may be stored in: QA Drive > Training Logs > YYYY
