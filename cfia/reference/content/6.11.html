Purpose

To ensure Fratello Coffee Roasters' premises are designed, maintained, and monitored in a way that prevents contamination, supports food safety and sanitation, and complies with CFIA's Safe Food for Canadians Regulations (SFCR) – Part 4 (Preventive Controls).

Scope

Applies to all interior and exterior areas of Fratello's premises, including:

  • Production and storage areas
  • Washrooms, staff spaces, lunchrooms
  • Maintenance and utility rooms
  • Exterior grounds, entryways, and surrounding site areas

Frequency

  • Monthly: Full interior and exterior premises inspection (documented)
  • Daily/Weekly: Spot inspections as part of Pre-Op, Sanitation, or Maintenance SOPs

Responsibility

Role Responsibility
Warehouse/Maintenance Supervisor (or designate) Leads monthly inspections and records findings
QA or Sanitation Lead Verifies corrective actions and follow-ups
All Staff Report facility defects or GMP issues observed

Procedure Overview

  • Conduct inspection using the Interior and Exterior Premises Inspection Records (Excel file)
  • Review last month's records before beginning to ensure all previous deviations were corrected
  • Complete walk-through of:
    • All indoor production, storage, utility, and staff areas
    • Entire external building perimeter, waste zones, traffic routes
  • Document deviations, assign corrective actions, and follow up
  • Reference supporting SOPs as needed (Pest Control, Returned Product, Glass Breakage)

Interior Premises Inspection – Key Areas

Area Checklist Focus
Storage Areas Materials off floor/walls, FIFO rotation, racking integrity
Building Condition Clean walls, sealed joints, no mold, pooling water, sealed doors
Ventilation Clean ducts, no dust buildup, proper spacing for production
Lighting Functioning bulbs, covered fixtures, fly bulbs OK
Equipment Clean, good condition, no tape/cardboard, ID labels intact
Drains Covered, cleaned regularly
Staff Areas Hand wash stations stocked, clean washrooms, tidy lunchroom
Tools & Utensils Properly stored, labeled by use, off floor
GMP Practices Jewelry policy, no personal items/food, proper glove use

Exterior Premises Inspection – Key Areas

Area Checklist Focus
Grounds & Neighbors No pest-attracting conditions, no flooding or spills
External Surfaces Sealed walls/pipes/vents, no pest openings, locked doors
Traffic Routes Maintained surface, no potholes, no dust hazard
Waste Storage Bins not overflowing, grounds clean, no loose debris

Corrective Actions

  • All deviations must be logged with description and resolution date
  • If product or material is compromised, follow the Defective or Suspect Product SOP
  • If pests or signs of infestation are observed, follow the Pest Control SOP
  • If glass or brittle plastic is damaged, follow the Glass Breakage SOP
  • All corrective actions are verified by QA and/or maintenance
