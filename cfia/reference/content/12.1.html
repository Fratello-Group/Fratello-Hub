1.0 Purpose

To define the procedure for packaging bulk loose leaf tea into Idle Tea retail units, ensuring traceability, product integrity, labeling accuracy, and compliance with CFIA preventive control and inventory management requirements.

2.0 Scope

This SOP applies to:

  • All bulk loose leaf tea sourced from Sarjesa
  • Packaging, inspection, and labeling of Idle Tea products
  • Inventory entry into Odoo ERP system

3.0 Responsibilities

RoleResponsibility
QA / Packaging OperatorEnsures machine prep, labeling verification, and packaging hygiene
Production SupervisorVerifies correct lot use and signs off inventory reconciliation
Inventory AdminInputs finished bag count into Odoo and flags partial lots

4.0 Procedure

4.1 Product Identity & Verification

  • Confirm tea supplier is Sarjesa
  • Match the product name on 15kg bulk bag to the correct Idle Tea retail product SKU
  • Log tea name, lot number (if provided), and date received into Inventory Intake Log
  • Perform a visual inspection of the 15kg bulk bag:
    • Ensure bag is fully intact, without any rips, tears, or signs of compromise
    • Document any packaging issues in QA notes and notify supervisor if found

4.2 Equipment Pre-Inspection

  • Perform a visual inspection of:
    • Hopper
    • Sealer
    • Bagging machine components
  • Remove any debris or foreign materials
  • Use a dry clean cloth to wipe the hopper thoroughly before tea is added
  • Document completion of inspection on Pre-Operational Equipment Log (6.3a)

4.3 Packaging Process

  • Pour the entire 15kg bulk tea into the cleaned hopper
  • Begin packaging Idle Tea units
  • Monitor seal integrity and label placement throughout the run
  • Ensure labels match the SKU and display correct name, weight, and lot
  • During scaling and packaging:
    • Conduct a visual inspection of the tea on the scale for any visible foreign matter
    • Remove any anomalies found and document if contamination is suspected
  • If any tea remains that doesn't complete a full bag, place it into a clean sealed container, label it “Partial Bag - To Hold,” and notify Inventory Admin for recording

4.4 Post-Packaging – Count & Inventory

  • Count all finished bags
  • Multiply by 10 to confirm how many full cases are assembled
  • Enter bag and case counts into Odoo ERP → Inventory / Make to Stock module
  • Move full cases to designated Idle Tea warehouse zone
  • Place any partial tea in 'Hold Area' for future packaging, marked with date and contents

5.0 Records and Forms

  • Pre-Operational Equipment Log (6.3a)
  • Inventory Intake Log
  • Packaging Batch Record (optional)
  • Odoo Inventory Adjustment Log
