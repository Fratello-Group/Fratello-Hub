Document Title: Import Control PCP — Sub-element: 1.3 – Import Control Documentation

1. Purpose

To ensure that imported materials such as green coffee beans and packaging materials comply with Canadian food safety laws by implementing a robust supplier approval and verification system, in accordance with SFCR Part 4 (Sections 47–81).

2. Scope

This procedure applies to all imported green coffee beans, packaging materials, cleaning chemicals, or any ingredient (if applicable) used in food contact or production at Fratello Coffee Roasters.

4.2. Monitoring and Re-approval

  • Annual review of all approved suppliers
  • Re-review if a deviation occurs (e.g., contamination, shipping issue)
  • Document corrective actions taken if any issue arises

8. Corrective Actions

If supplier fails to meet specification or COA is missing/invalid:

  • Product placed on Hold
  • QA initiates a Corrective Action Report
  • Supplier notified, issue reviewed
  • Product not used until risk assessed

9. Examples to Include in PCP Binder (CFIA-Ready)

  • Two recent COAs from imported green coffee (e.g., Ochratoxin A, moisture)
  • Approved Supplier List
  • GFSI or other 3rd-party audit certificate from a green bean exporter
  • Product spec sheet (green coffee origin, grade, defect tolerance)
  • Shipping record + lot number from Cropster
  • Receiving and Inspection Log with importer name
