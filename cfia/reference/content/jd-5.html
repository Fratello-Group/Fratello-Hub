Summary

The Warehouse Supervisor runs the day-to-day operations of the Fratello warehouse — purchasing and receiving all supplies (excluding green coffee), packaging, pick/pack, shipping and delivery — ensuring Analog Café, wholesale and online customer orders are delivered accurately and on time.

Job titleWarehouse Supervisor
Reports toOperations Manager
Direct reportsWarehouse Lead, Packaging Machine Operator, Packaging Team Members, Delivery Drivers

Warehouse management

  • Oversee daily warehouse activities — planning, scheduling and leading the packaging and warehouse team; enter payroll.
  • Source, buy, receive and maintain all inventory (excluding green coffee) for Analog Cafés, online and wholesale customers; forecast inventory needs and maintain a PAR system for all SKUs.
  • Develop and negotiate supplier and shipping contracts; evaluate and source carriers for cost savings.
  • Schedule and coordinate all outbound shipments; complete packing slips and bills of lading; ensure every item has an invoice and meets standards.
  • Set and maintain preventive-maintenance standards and schedules for packaging equipment, scales, compressor and grinders; conduct regular quality checks and confirm scales are accurately calibrated.
  • Train and coach the team on SOPs and safety; complete performance evaluations; oversee the sanitation schedule and a tidy yard.

Inventory management

  • Oversee inventory-control procedures and accuracy goals; keep policy and procedure documentation current.
  • Receive, inventory and warehouse all orders accurately; distribute inventory to the correct production area.
  • Lead inventory integrity and counts (daily, monthly, quarterly, annually); create purchase orders for everything except green coffee; enter requisitions, POs and packing slips into QuickBooks; verify receipts against POs and supplier invoices.

Monitor & report

  • Monitor inventory levels (excluding green coffee) and order accordingly; analyze and report warehouse KPIs to leadership.
  • Maintain accurate records of incoming/outgoing shipments; develop warehouse scorecards for continuous improvement of quality, efficiency and inventory accuracy.
  • Ensure appropriate staffing for volume; create and continuously improve wholesale/online packaging standards; maintain a shipment process that sends tracking to customers once an order ships.
