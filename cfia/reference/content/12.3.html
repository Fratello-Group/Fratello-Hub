1.0 Purpose

To establish a system for documenting and verifying the receipt and inspection of all loose leaf tea ingredients supplied to Fratello Coffee Roasters for use in Idle Tea production. This supports traceability, supplier validation, and CFIA import control compliance.

2.0 Scope

This procedure applies to:

  • All loose leaf tea sourced from Sarjesa or other approved suppliers
  • Bulk tea packaging received for Idle Tea SKUs
  • Initial verification activities prior to use in production

3.0 Responsibilities

RoleResponsibility
QA CoordinatorReviews tea supplier files and ensures form completion
Receiving StaffInspects tea bags upon receipt and completes verification log
Inventory AdminEnters received weight and tea type into Odoo system

4.0 Procedure

Step 1 – Bulk Tea Receipt

  • Upon delivery, record the following on the Tea Supplier Verification Log (Form 12.3a):
    • Date received
    • Supplier name (e.g., Sarjesa)
    • Product name / tea blend
    • Weight received (kg)
    • Batch/lot number (if available)

Step 2 – Packaging Integrity Check

  • Perform a visual inspection of the bulk tea bag:
    • Ensure bag is intact and free from rips, tears, or punctures
    • Look for signs of moisture, infestation, or damage
  • If defects are found, notify QA and document in the Deviation Log

Step 3 – Tea Quality Confirmation

  • Optional: Record results of aroma, colour, or visual consistency
  • Identify and remove any visible foreign matter during unpacking (Step 1 of SOP 12.1)

Step 4 – Record Storage Location

  • Record where the tea is stored (e.g., Tea Inventory Rack, Warehouse B)
  • Enter tea data into Odoo for tracking and traceability

5.0 Records and Forms

  • Tea Supplier & Ingredient Verification Log (Form 12.3a)
  • Deviation Report (if applicable)
  • Inventory Intake Log
  • Odoo Product Master File
