Purpose

To ensure that all labels applied to food products by Fratello Coffee Roasters are accurate, compliant with Canadian regulations, match product specifications, and are verified before release for sale or distribution. This procedure supports compliance with SFCR Part 2 (Trade), Part 5 (Traceability), and Food Labelling for Industry Guidance.

Scope

Applies to all:

  • Retail and wholesale food labels (bags, boxes, outer cases)
  • Printed labels applied in-house (e.g., roast date, lot codes, nutrition, ingredients)
  • Pre-printed bags with embedded product or company claims

Responsibility

RoleResponsibility

Required Label Elements (Canada)

All food labels must include the following unless exempt:

  • Common name of the food
  • Net quantity (metric units)
  • Business name and contact (Fratello Coffee Roasters or "Prepared for")
  • Date markings (e.g., roast date or best before date, in format YYMMDD or MMDDYY)
  • Storage instructions (if applicable)
  • Lot code (used for traceability)
  • Ingredients and allergens (if applicable)
  • Bilingual content (English & French, unless exempt)

Label Approval and Version Control

  • All new or modified labels must be reviewed by QA and the Director of Operations.
  • Marketing/design team must submit digital proof for approval.
  • Final label file is stored in the Label Control Folder (digital master).
  • QA signs off using the Label Review Checklist (Appendix A).
  • A new version is logged in the Label Version Log, including:
    • Version number
    • Date approved
    • Purpose of change
    • Responsible party

Label Printing and Application Controls

  • CoPilot printer system is used for on-site roast date and lot coding.
  • Operator must verify:
    • Correct SKU and label file selected
    • Printer is calibrated and functioning
    • Test print matches approved label
  • Random checks every hour by line lead or QA (documented in Packaging Log)
  • Only trained and approved staff may print or apply labels

Label Storage and Access

  • Only current, approved label files may be printed or distributed
  • Old or outdated labels must be physically removed and destroyed
  • Label templates are version-controlled and stored in the digital Label Archive Folder
