Purpose

This SOP outlines the complete process for final product packaging, including manual packaging steps, labelling, sealing, and integrated traceability using Odoo ERP. It ensures that all final packaged products meet food safety, labelling, and regulatory compliance requirements under Fratello Coffee Roasters' Preventive Control Plan (PCP) and the Safe Food for Canadians Regulations (SFCR).

Scope

Applies to all Fratello packaging operators, line leads, and QA personnel involved in:

  • Setting up and operating sealing, boxing, and labelling equipment
  • Applying and verifying product labels
  • Recording product data in Odoo
  • Handling packaging deviations and ensuring traceability from roast lot to finished product

1. Packaging Line Setup (Manual Steps)

Tasks

  • Turn on sealing and boxing equipment (heat sealers, boxing tables, conveyor if applicable)
  • Ensure all food-contact surfaces are clean and sanitized (see Pre-Op SOP)
  • Calibrate or check scales for weight accuracy
  • Prepare packaging components: bags, degassers, boxes, labels
  • Verify all components match the roast product being packed

Documentation

  • Pre-Operational Inspection Checklist
  • Packaging Log (paper or Odoo-integrated)

Food Safety Verification

  • Bags and labels must be free from dust, debris, moisture
  • Only approved packaging materials from the Approved Supplier List are permitted

2. Bag Filling and Sealing

Tasks

  • Weigh coffee into bags based on pack size (e.g., 340g, 5lb)
  • Insert degasser if required
  • Confirm proper seal using heat sealer (test at start of shift)
  • Line lead performs random seal and weight checks hourly

Non-Conformances

  • Underweight/overweight bags are rejected and reworked
  • Bad seals must be re-sealed or discarded

3. Labelling Procedure

Tasks

  • Use CoPilot or pre-approved label files for:
    • Roast date (YYMMDD)
    • Lot number (linked to roast batch)
    • Origin, weight, contact details, bilingual elements
  • Confirm label matches approved design from Label Version Log
  • Apply to bag before or after sealing, depending on flow

Odoo Integration

  • Odoo MO must reflect:
    • Roast lot → Packaging MO → Finished product lot
    • Packaging component lots used (e.g., bag SKU, label SKU)

Label QA Checks

  • First label every batch is signed off by QA
  • Hourly random label checks logged in QA Checklist
  • Errors = hold product, re-label, report under Corrective Action Procedure

4. Boxing, Taping, and Inventory Completion

Tasks

  • Group sealed bags by SKU into pre-labeled boxes
  • Add case label with finished lot number and roast date
  • Tape box and apply shipping label if required
  • Update physical or digital log of quantity boxed
  • Move to finished goods inventory in Odoo

Odoo Completion

  • Finalize MO
  • Ensure lot traceability is complete:
    • Green Bean Lot → Roast Lot → Packaging Lot → Inventory

Traceability Tip: Use Odoo Smart Buttons ("Used In" / "Where Used") to verify links.

5. Deviations and Corrective Actions

Issue Action
Mislabelled product Isolate and re-label; log in CAR and Hold Log
Incorrect lot code Notify QA; trace back roast; rework or dispose
Packaging failure (seal, weight) Rework or discard per QA instructions

CFIA Compliance Highlights

  • Complies with SFCR Part 4 (Preventive Controls – Processing, Labelling, Packaging)
  • Satisfies traceability under SFCR Part 5 (one step forward, one step back)
  • Integrated with personnel training, sanitation, and packaging supplier controls
