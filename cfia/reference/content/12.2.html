1.0 Purpose

To establish a verification process that ensures Idle Tea packaging is labeled correctly, accurately reflects the product inside, and complies with Canadian food labeling regulations, including traceability and preventive control requirements under SFCR.

2.0 Scope

This procedure applies to:

  • All Idle Tea SKUs produced and packaged at Fratello Coffee Roasters
  • Pre-printed and digitally printed labels (including CoPilot system output)
  • Cross-verification of bulk tea received and retail packaging specifications

3.0 Responsibilities

RoleResponsibility
QA CoordinatorMaintains label templates and verifies batch-to-label accuracy
Packaging OperatorVerifies label during packaging and reports mismatches
Inventory AdminConfirms final lot traceability and packaging SKU accuracy in Odoo

4.0 Procedure

Step 1 – Label Verification Before Packaging

  • Match bulk tea product name on 15kg Sarjesa bag to the correct Idle Tea SKU
  • Retrieve the corresponding approved label file or printed label stock
  • Confirm the following are accurate:
    • Product name (as sold)
    • Net weight (e.g., 100g, 250g)
    • Lot number (manual or from system)
    • “Packaged on” or “Best Before” date
    • Company name, address, and contact info
    • French/English compliance (bilingual)
  • If using the CoPilot system, print a sample label, attach to batch record, and verify before starting full run

Step 2 – During Packaging

  • QA or Operator performs spot checks every 25–50 units:
    • Confirm correct label is applied
    • Ensure alignment and legibility on front and back of the pouch
    • Check that all variable info (lot, date) is present and clear
  • Record verification on Tea Packaging Batch Record (Form 12.4)

Step 3 – Final Verification & Traceability

  • Before closing each packaging run:
    • Count and confirm total units match labels used
    • Ensure Odoo reflects the correct SKU and packaging date
    • Ensure each batch is traceable from Sarjesa lot to retail pack
  • Label verification is subject to Internal Audit (SOP 1.3)

5.0 Records and Forms

  • Tea Packaging Batch Record (Form 12.4)
  • Label Verification Checklist (optional)
  • CoPilot Sample Label Printout
  • Odoo Inventory Record
