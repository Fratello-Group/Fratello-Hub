Summary

The Coffee Roaster roasts Fratello Coffee Roasters' high-quality coffee, maintains coffee-bean inventory and equipment preventive maintenance, and works with the Lead Coffee Roaster / Green Coffee Buyer and Quality Assurance Manager to ensure coffee is roasted to specification — detail-oriented, efficient, and focused on quality and consistency.

Job titleCoffee Roaster
Reports toDirector of Coffee / Director of Production
Direct reportsn/a

Coffee roasting

  • Complete the daily roasting requirements on the schedule; assist in creating and implementing the weekly roasting schedule.
  • Maintain accurate inventory: retrieve the correct green beans per the recipe and record weights accurately in Cropster.
  • Roast following the recipe and roast profile in Cropster; inspect the roast regularly; confirm roasted beans match the colour specifications and roast curve.
  • Work with the Quality Assurance Manager to adjust roast specifications; record the final roasted weight in Cropster to track shrinkage.
  • Unload roasted coffee into accurately marked bins; complete a Build Assembly in Odoo after every roast (before the next) so packaging can bag it; mark the build-assembly number on the tote tag and production sheet.
  • Participate in Quality Assurance activities.

Organization, cleanliness & maintenance

  • Follow inventory-management procedures; conduct regular cleaning and maintenance of roasting equipment.
  • Monitor the roasting process for maintenance issues (warped drums, burner inconsistencies, electrical) and report them to the Lead Roaster / Green Coffee Buyer.
  • Observe machine operation for malfunctions; follow safety procedures and report hazards and incidents to leadership.
  • Perform preventive maintenance daily, weekly and monthly per the operations standards; follow food-handling and sanitation standards; assist Packaging Team Members as required.

Experience & qualifications

  • Must love coffee! 1+ years as a machine operator (food environment preferred).
  • High accuracy and attention to detail; effective communication and time-management; thrives in a team.
  • Proficient with Microsoft Word/Excel/Outlook and QuickBooks (or comparable); minimum high-school education.
  • Forklift certification; heavy lifting required.
